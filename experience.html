Experience

The best way to gain it is to act as if you don't have it.

Throughout my life I have had a number of opportunities to try new things. Working as a missionary is one thing, and sitting at a desk is another. What I look forward to now is the chance to settle down and apply myself to one profession, learning and growing as I go. What follows is a list of the experiences I have had and what I have gained from them.

  1. Full Time missionary - Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. Brazil Campinas Mission
      How to lead over a group of people in a way that I can magnify their strengths.
      How to dedicate not just the work day to my task, but every moment of my life for two years.
      The self mastery needed to not just do difficult things, but put off simple comforts
  2. Farm Hand - Brasher Farm
      The importance in hard work until the task is finished and not a moment sooner
  3. Helu Multifamily Real Estate LLC - Manual Labor, Technology Reseach/Application, Accounting

In my free time I went to where we were doing rennovations and helped with demolitions to make sure I was always doing something.
      How to read, record, and report important financial information for the company.
